HOW TO ADD IN CLOUDFLARE DASHBOARD
================================================================================

1. Go to: https://dash.cloudflare.com/a313f914a3caf00d68b03de5eadc54cc/pages/view/forger-digital/settings

2. Scroll to "Environment Variables" section

3. For each variable:
   - Click "Add variable"
   - Enter Variable name (e.g., "DATABASE_URL")
   - Enter Value (the actual value)
   - Select environment: "Production" or "Preview" (add to BOTH)
   - Click "Save"

4. After adding all variables, click "Save" at the bottom

5. Go to "Deployments" tab and redeploy
